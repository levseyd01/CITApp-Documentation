## Filter by Status

The {column}`Status` columnindicates the current status of the account.

Possible {item-blue}`Status` options include:

- {item}`All`
- {item-green}`Active`
- {item-cyan}`Pending Activation`
- {item}`Inactive`
- {item-red}`Locked Out`
