Each card will display information about a document, including:

- {item-blue}`File Name`
- {item-blue}`File Type`
- {item-blue}`Date` and {item-blue}`Time Uploaded`
- {item-blue}`Uploaded By`
- {item-blue}`Status`
- (If Applicable) {item-blue}`Approved By`
- {item-blue}`Messages`
